# Day 4: Demo & interview preparation

## Learning objectives

- Deliver a **10-minute** narrative: problem → metrics → architecture → tradeoffs.
- Prepare **20** STAR-style stories (system design, conflict, failure, ethics).

## Hands-on

1. Record yourself (voice memo) doing the demo — tighten script.
2. List **follow-up questions** you’d ask an interviewer about their stack.
3. Flash-card **tradeoffs**: FAISS vs managed, fine-tune vs RAG, agents vs single chain.

## Checklist

- [ ] One diagram you can draw on a whiteboard from memory in 3 minutes.
- [ ] Three **failure** stories with metrics and resolution.
- [ ] Honest limitations of your capstone (interviewers respect self-awareness).
