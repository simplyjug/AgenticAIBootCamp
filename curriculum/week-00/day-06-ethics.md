# Day 6: Ethics & Responsible AI

## Theory: Ethical AI Practices

AI must be fair, transparent, and safe. Ethics covers bias, privacy, and societal impact.

### Key Concepts
- **Bias**: Unfair outcomes from data.
- **Transparency**: Explainable AI decisions.
- **Privacy**: Protecting user data.
- **Regulation**: Laws like GDPR, AI Act.

### Best Practices
- Audit models for bias.
- Use diverse data.
- Be transparent about limitations.
